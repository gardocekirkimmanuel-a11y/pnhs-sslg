About PNHS SSLG

The PNHS SSLG serves as the official student governing body representing the voices, welfare, and aspirations of every learner.

The organization promotes student leadership, discipline, service, innovation, and active participation through impactful programs and learner-centered initiatives.

Guided by integrity and excellence, the SSLG continuously strives to build a more empowered, united, and progressive school community.
