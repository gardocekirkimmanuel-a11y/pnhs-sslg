About PNHS SSLG

The Puti-an National High School Supreme Secondary Learner Government serves as the official student governing body dedicated to representing the voices, interests, and welfare of every learner.

Through meaningful leadership and collaborative governance, the SSLG creates impactful initiatives that promote academic growth, discipline, student empowerment, and community participation.

Guided by integrity, service, and excellence, the organization continuously strives to establish learner-centered programs that create positive and sustainable change within the school community.

Strategic Goals

Proteksyon

Ensuring learner safety, welfare, inclusivity, and student support systems across the school community.

Pag-unlad

Promoting leadership development, academic excellence, innovation, and holistic learner growth.

Pagpapahalaga

Strengthening respect, unity, discipline, appreciation, and active participation among learners.
