About PNHS SSLG

The official student governing body representing the interests, welfare, aspirations, and leadership of every learner at Puti-an National High School.

The PNHS SSLG promotes student leadership, active participation, discipline, innovation, and learner empowerment through impactful and learner-centered initiatives.

The organization continuously creates programs that strengthen collaboration, unity, school improvement, and holistic learner development.

Guided by integrity, excellence, and service, the SSLG strives to become a catalyst for positive change and student representation.

Strategic Goals

Proteksyon

Ensuring learner safety, welfare, inclusivity, and accessible support systems.

Pag-unlad

Promoting academic excellence, leadership growth, innovation, and holistic development.

Pagpapahalaga

Strengthening respect, unity, discipline, appreciation, and active participation.
