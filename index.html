About PNHS SSLG

The PNHS SSLG serves as the official student government that represents learners and promotes leadership, welfare, and unity within the school community.

It is committed to developing responsible student leaders who advocate for student rights, academic growth, and school improvement initiatives.

Guided by service and excellence, the organization strives to create meaningful programs that empower every learner to become active contributors to society.
