About PNHS SSLG

The Puti-an National High School Supreme Secondary Learner Government serves as the official student governing body representing the interests, voices, and welfare of every learner.

Through leadership, collaboration, and innovation, the SSLG promotes student empowerment, discipline, active participation, and learner-centered initiatives.

Guided by service and excellence, the organization continuously develops impactful programs that strengthen unity, leadership, and school improvement.

Strategic Goals

Proteksyon

Ensuring learner safety, inclusivity, student welfare, and accessible support systems.

Pag-unlad

Promoting academic excellence, leadership growth, innovation, and holistic development.

Pagpapahalaga

Strengthening respect, unity, discipline, appreciation, and active participation.
